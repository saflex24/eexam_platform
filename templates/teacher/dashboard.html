Student Reports - Complete Guide
What are Student Reports?

Professional, print-ready exam reports that include:

  • School Information: Logo, name, and contact details
  • Student Details: Name, admission number, and class
  • Exam Information: Title, subject, date, and duration
  • Performance Summary: Marks, percentage, grade, and status
  • Question Analysis: Detailed breakdown of each question with correct/incorrect indicators
How to Generate Reports
  1. Find the exam in your dashboard
  2. Click the button
  3. You'll see a list of all students who took the exam
  4. Choose one of these options:
    • Individual Report: Click download button next to student name
    • Bulk Download: Click "Download All Reports (ZIP)"
    • Selected Students: Check boxes and click "Download Selected"
Printing Tips
  • Reports are optimized for standard Letter or A4 paper
  • Each report is maximum 4 pages
  • Works with any printer (color or black & white)
  • Consider duplex (double-sided) printing to save paper
  • Multiple reports can be printed at once from ZIP file
Customization

To customize your reports with school branding:

  1. Edit the get_school_settings() function in routes/teacher.py
  2. Update school name, address, phone, and email
  3. Add your school logo to static/uploads/school_logo.png
  4. Reports will automatically use your branding
Report Features
For Multiple Choice:
  • [CORRECT] = Right answer
  • [X WRONG] = Student's wrong choice
  • All options are shown
For Theory Questions:
  • Student's answer displayed
  • Expected answer shown
  • Marks awarded per question
Need Help?

If you encounter any issues, check that Node.js is installed (node --version) and that the docx library is available (npm list -g docx). See the setup documentation for detailed troubleshooting.
